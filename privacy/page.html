Unfixed Studio ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or engage with us in other ways.

Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways, including when you visit our website, interact with our content, or use our services. The types of information we collect may include:

Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.

Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, location data, device information, and usage data.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

To provide, maintain, and improve our services.

To communicate with you, respond to inquiries, and send relevant updates.

To personalize and optimize your experience on our website.

To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners and service providers who assist us in delivering services. These third parties are required to handle your information securely and only for the purposes we specify. We may also share your information in the following situations:

If required by law or in response to legal requests.

To protect the rights, property, or safety of Unfixed Studio, our users, or the public.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. While we use commercially reasonable means to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

Your Rights and Choices

Access: You can request a copy of the information we have about you.

Correction: You can correct or update any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain limitations.

Opt-out of Communications: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize your preferences. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing them with your personal information.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a revised "Effective Date."
